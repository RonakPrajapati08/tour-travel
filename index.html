The Founder's Story

A Journey That Started with One Trek

"I didn't find my purpose in a classroom. I found it on a trail in Polo Forest at age 22."

In 2012, Arjun Patel quit his corporate job and spent three months walking through the forests of Gujarat and the mountains of Himachal. What he discovered wasn't just beautiful landscapes — it was a deep hunger in ordinary people to connect with nature, but no trusted guide to take them there.

WildRoots began with a single WhatsApp group, 12 friends, and a rented tempo. Today it has grown into Gujarat's most loved adventure tour company — with a team of 35 certified guides, 50+ curated routes, and over 4,800 lives transformed.
